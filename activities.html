From Wheels to Ice & Snow

Did you know that **roller skating** is one of the best ways to prepare for **ice skating** and **snow skiing**? The balance, movements, and techniques are similar, making the transition **smooth and easy**.

We offer **beginner group lessons** for both **ice skating** and **snow skiing** to help you explore these exciting sports with confidence!

⛸ Ice Skating Beginner Lessons

Who is this for? Beginners with or without roller skating experience.

What You Will Learn:

  • ✅ Basic balance and stance on ice
  • ✅ Gliding, stopping, and turning techniques
  • ✅ Understanding edge control and movement
  • ✅ Fun drills and confidence-building exercises

Class Details:

🕒 Duration: 1 hour

👥 Group Size: Up to 10 students

📍 Location: Local ice rink (to be announced)

🎿 Snow Skiing Beginner Lessons

Who is this for? First-time skiers who want a fun introduction to the sport.

What You Will Learn:

  • ✅ Proper stance and weight distribution on skis
  • ✅ Basic movement, turns, and stopping techniques
  • ✅ Introduction to small slopes and ski control

Class Details:

🕒 Duration: Half-day lesson (3–4 hours)

👥 Group Size: 8–12 students

📍 Location: Ski resort (to be announced)
