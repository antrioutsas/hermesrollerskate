Start Your Roller Skating Journey in Athens

Welcome to Hermes Rollerskate Academy, where children and adults learn to skate in a safe and fun environment in Zografou, Athens. Are you a complete beginner? Book a private roller skating lesson to build confidence and master the basics—rolling, balance, and safe stops. Already know how to skate? Join our group roller skating classes in Athens and develop your freestyle, speed, or dance skills. Don't have your own roller skates or protection?
