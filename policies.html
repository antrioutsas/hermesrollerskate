📜 Policies & Information

We are committed to providing a safe and enjoyable skating experience. Below are our key policies regarding participation, safety, refunds, and privacy.

📑 Terms & Conditions

By participating in our academy, you agree to the following terms:

  • ✔ **Lesson Participation** – Lessons must be **prepaid** and scheduled in advance.
  • ✔ **Respectful Behavior** – All skaters must maintain a **positive and respectful environment**.
  • ✔ **Liability Waiver** – Skaters participate **at their own risk**. The academy is not responsible for injuries.
  • ✔ **Mandatory Equipment** – Helmets, knee pads, and elbow pads are required during lessons.

🔐 Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and ensure **your data is protected**.

  • ✔ **What We Collect** – Basic details (name, phone, email) for lesson registrations.
  • ✔ **How We Use Data** – Only for **academy communications** (no third-party sharing).
  • ✔ **Your Rights** – You can request to **access, update, or delete** your data anytime.

💰 Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • ✔ **Lesson Prepayment Required** – To reserve your spot.
  • ✔ **Missed Lessons** – Missing 3 lessons without notice results in **one lesson hour penalty**.
  • ✔ **Workshop Refunds** – 50% refund if canceled **before the session starts**.
  • ✔ **Merchandise Reservations** – If an item is reserved and not picked up, future reservations may be restricted.

🛡️ Safety Guidelines

  • ✔ **Protective Gear Required** – Helmets, knee pads, and elbow pads are mandatory.
  • ✔ **Proper Skate Maintenance** – Skates must be in **good condition** before training.
  • ✔ **Instructor Supervision** – Follow coach instructions at all times.
  • ✔ **Weather Cancellations** – Lessons are suspended in case of **rain, wet court, or temperatures below 5°C**.
  • ✔ **First Aid Available** – Our instructors are trained in **basic first aid procedures**.
