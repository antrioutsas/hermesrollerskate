Attendance & Payment Rules

❗ Lesson Prepayment: All lessons are prepaid to secure a spot.

📅 No-Show Policy: If a student misses 3 lessons without prior notice, they lose 1 lesson hour.

🔄 Workshop Cancellation: Workshops are prepaid 100%, and if missed, students receive a 50% refund.

Weather & Cancellations

Lessons will be **suspended** if:

  • 🌧 **Rainy or wet courts**
  • ❄ **Temperatures below 5°C**
  • ⚠ **Unsafe skating conditions**

Students will be notified in advance if lessons are postponed.

Need Equipment?

If you don’t have roller skates or protective gear, let us know in advance. We’ll provide them if available.
