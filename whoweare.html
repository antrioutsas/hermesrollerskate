Who We Are

Welcome to Hermes Roller Skating Academy, a place where passion for skating meets expert coaching. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced skater, we are here to guide you on your journey to mastering roller skating.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the joy of roller skating, providing a fun and safe environment for all ages. We believe in fostering skills, confidence, and a sense of community through our structured training programs.

What Makes Us Different?

  • 🏆 Expert Coaches: Certified and experienced trainers.
  • 🛼 Diverse Classes: From beginners to freestyle, racing, and dancing.
  • 🌍 Community & Events: Skating meetups, competitions, and city tours.
  • 🎓 Progressive Training: Learn at your own pace with personalized coaching.
